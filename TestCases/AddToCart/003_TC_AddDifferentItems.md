# Test Case: Add Different Products to Cart

## Test Case ID
TC_CART_003

## Objective
To verify that a user can add different products to the cart and each appears as a separate line item.

## Preconditions
- User is able to access at least two different product detail pages.
- All products are in stock.

## Test Data
Product A: In-stock product  
Product B: Different in-stock product

## Test Steps
1. Open the product detail page for Product A.
2. Click "Add to Cart".
3. Open the product detail page for Product B.
4. Click "Add to Cart".
5. Open the cart page.
6. Observe cart contents.

## Expected Result
- Both Product A and Product B should appear in the cart as separate items.
- Each product should show correct name, price and quantity.
- Cart total should be the sum of both products.

## Status
Not Executed
